Corporate Governance

We are committed to the highest standards of corporate governance, transparency, and ethical business practices.

Our Governance Framework

DHI India operates under a robust corporate governance framework that ensures accountability, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our business activities.

Our governance structure is designed to protect the interests of all stakeholders while enabling effective decision-making and long-term value creation.

We adhere to the corporate governance standards prescribed under Indian law and maintain alignment with the governance practices of our parent organization, Druk Holding & Investments.

"Good governance is not about rules and regulations. It's about creating an environment where ethical behavior is the norm and where all stakeholders can trust that their interests are being protected."
