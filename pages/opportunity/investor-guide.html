Investor Guide

Learn about co-investment opportunities and partnership models with DHI India.

Investment Opportunities

DHI India offers co-investment opportunities for strategic partners who share our vision of strengthening Bhutan-India economic ties.

We seek partners with complementary expertise, patient capital, and a long-term perspective on value creation.

Our investment approach combines the stability of sovereign wealth management with the agility of private capital to identify and execute attractive opportunities.

Partnership Models

01

Direct Co-Investment

Participate alongside DHI India in specific investment opportunities with clear governance and exit structures.

02

Strategic Partnerships

Long-term partnerships for market access, technology transfer, and operational collaboration.

03

Joint Ventures

Create new ventures combining DHI India's network with partner expertise in specific sectors.

Focus Sectors

Infrastructure

Power generation, renewable energy, and fuel distribution.

Natural Resources

Mining, minerals, and resource processing.

Agro-Processing

Industrial production and value-added manufacturing.

Technology
