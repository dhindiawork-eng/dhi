Careers at DHI India

Join our team and be part of building bridges between Bhutan and India. We're looking for passionate individuals who share our vision.

Why Join DHI India?

At DHI India, you'll have the opportunity to work on meaningful projects that strengthen economic ties between two great nations.

We offer a collaborative work environment, competitive compensation, and the chance to be part of a pioneering venture that is shaping the future of Bhutan-India economic cooperation.

Whether you're an experienced professional or just starting your career, we have opportunities for growth and development.
